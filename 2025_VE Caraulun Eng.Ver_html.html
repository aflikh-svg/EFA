📋 Preliminary Value Engineering Report

Caraulun River Water Resources Development Scheme, Manufahi, Timor-Leste

Feasibility Study · Dec 2025 · Executive Summary (VE Synthesis)

🔍 1. Background & Objectives

The Caraulun River Basin faces extreme seasonal water paradox: >2,000 mm/year rainfall but dry season discharge below 1 m³/s (Q80). Existing irrigation serves <30% of potential land (>3,500 ha). This VE report aims to optimize function, cost, and sustainability for government decision-making, focusing on scenario S3 (multipurpose dam) while comparing S0–S3 alternatives.

🎯 Main VE objectives: analyze basic functions, compare technical/social/environmental criteria, identify innovations (design, materials, O&M), and provide measurable recommendations.

📐 2. Methodology (SAVE International Job Plan)

6-phase structured approach: Information Gathering → Function Analysis (FAST & cost-function) → Creative (brainstorming 18+ ideas) → Evaluation → Development → Presentation. Life-cycle cost, risk-based assessment, and multidisciplinary team (17 specialists).

💡 3. Creative Ideas & Screening

🌊 For all scenarios: smart water metering, precision agriculture, results-based conservation incentives, farmer water user associations (WUA).
🏗️ S3 optimization: Dam height/geometry optimization, “design for deconstruction”, e-flow for environmental health, livelihood restoration scheme, indigenous co-management (Tara Bandu).

✔️ Top high-priority ideas selected: Small reservoir cascade (S2), Results-Based Conservation, Dam optimization (type & height), Proactive Livelihood Restoration.

📦 5. Alternative Packages (A, B, C)

  • Package A (Cascade system – S2 evolved): Moderate CAPEX (USD 85-105M), irrigation ~2,200 ha, low social impact, good balance.
  • Package B (Optimized Sui Dam – S3): CAPEX USD 100-115M, irrigation 2,800-3,000 ha, max flood control, highest value if socio-environmental risks managed.
  • Package C (Rehabilitation + conservation): USD 25-40M, limited reliability, quick-win but does not solve storage.
✅ MAIN RECOMMENDATION (VE TEAM): Adopt Package B – “Optimized & Responsible Sui Dam” with specifications:
▪ Location: Sui Dam Case-3
▪ Dam type: Rockfill Dam
▪ Mandatory integrated plans: Social Management (livelihood restoration) + Environmental Management (e-flow, MPA monitoring).

📋 Project Delivery Options (preliminary view)

Based on the VE analysis, Design-Build/Turnkey offers cost certainty and faster delivery for Package B. BOT/PPP could be considered for the micro-hydro component to attract private financing.

📅 Next Steps & Integration

Jan–Feb 2026: Detailed geotechnical investigation at Case-3, finalize social census. Mar: Rockfill conceptual design. Apr: ESIA & management plans. May–Jun: Updated financial analysis + final FS report integrating VE recommendations.

Value Equation: VALUE = FUNCTION / COST — This VE process demonstrates that Case-3 Rockfill dam delivers highest long-term benefits (irrigation, flood control, hydropower, water security) with manageable risk, providing optimal value for Timor-Leste.

🔍 1. Latar Belakang & Tujuan

DAS Caraulun mengalami paradoks air ekstrem: curah hujan >2.000 mm/tahun namun debit musim kemarau (Q80) <1 m³/dtk. Irigasi eksisting melayani <30% lahan potensial (>3.500 ha). Laporan VE ini bertujuan mengoptimalkan fungsi, biaya, dan keberlanjutan untuk pengambilan keputusan pemerintah, dengan fokus pada skenario S3 (dam serbaguna) serta pembanding S0–S3.

🎯 Tujuan utama VE: menganalisis fungsi dasar, membandingkan kriteria teknis/sosial/lingkungan, identifikasi inovasi (desain, material, operasi), dan rekomendasi terukur.

📐 2. Metodologi (SAVE International Job Plan)

6 fase terstruktur: Pengumpulan Informasi → Analisis Fungsi (FAST & biaya-fungsi) → Kreatif (brainstorming 18+ ide) → Evaluasi → Pengembangan → Presentasi. Mempertimbangkan biaya siklus hidup, analisis risiko, tim multidisiplin (17 ahli).

💡 3. Ide Kreatif & Penyaringan

🌊 Untuk semua skenario: pengukuran air pintar, pertanian presisi, insentif konservasi berbasis hasil, P3A (perkumpulan petani pemakai air).
🏗️ Optimasi S3: optimalisasi tinggi/geometri dam, “design for deconstruction”, e-flow ekosistem sungai, skema pemulihan mata pencaharian, tata kelola bersama masyarakat adat (Tara Bandu).

✔️ Ide prioritas tinggi: sistem kaskade waduk kecil (S2), konservasi berbasis insentif, optimasi tipe & tinggi dam, restorasi mata pencaharian proaktif.
