📊 Value Engineering Summary Report

Warsamson Dam Project – Sorong District, West Papua Province

Detail Engineering Design Phase | 2022

📌 Project Overview

Location: Warsamson River, Sorong District, West Papua.
Catchment Area: 1,340.55 km².
Irrigation Potential: 15.5 m³/s  |  Raw Water: 8.19 m³/s.

A structured Value Engineering (VE) process was applied during the Detail Engineering Design phase, covering Information, Creative, Analysis & Development, and Recommendation phases.

💡 Creative Phase – Key Alternatives

Three dam types were evaluated based on topography and geology:

  • Earth-core Rockfill Dam
  • Concrete Faced Rockfill Dam (CFRD)
  • Concrete Gravity Dam (CGD)

Other creative aspects: dam height optimization, hydropower discharge, number of units, penstock size, and diversion scheme.

⚙️ Analysis & Development Phase – Key Decisions

✅ Dam Site Alternative-3 selected as most favorable after weighted scoring.
✅ Dam Type Concrete Gravity Dam (CGD) – narrow valley with steep abutments, sound rock, favorable spillway & intake layout.
✅ Dam Height Crest level El. 62 m selected (lowest cost and favorable economic indicators).
Cost comparison: El. 62 m = IDR 4,691.37 billion; El. 69 m = IDR 5,049.49 billion; El. 75 m = IDR 5,635.59 billion.
✅ Hydropower Discharge Optimized using energy optimization curve (1999–2020 flow data).
✅ Number of Units 3-unit powerhouse selected (higher annual energy & revenue vs. 2 units). Tariff: 10.23 cent/kWh.
✅ Penstock Diameter 4.0 m optimum diameter from revenue vs. construction cost trade-off.
✅ Diversion Scheme Diversion via tunnels (Scheme 1) preferred over diversion channel.

💰 Economic & Financial Comparison – Recommended Design

Concrete Gravity Dam (CGD) was significantly more cost-effective:

Concrete Gravity Dam (CGD) – Selected Base (lowest cost)
Alternative-2 +358,128.56 million
Alternative-3 +944,222.19 million

Economic indicators (ENPV, EBCR) improved with height up to El. 62 m, with benefits exceeding costs.

📌 Conclusions & Recommendations

  • Dam type: Concrete Gravity Dam (CGD) – most economical.
  • Crest level: El. 62 m – lowest cost & favorable economic returns.
  • Powerhouse: 3 units, design discharge 102 m³/s, penstock 4.0 m diameter.
  • Diversion: Tunnel system.
  • Optimization of hydropower discharge, units, penstock, and diversion resulted in significant cost savings without compromising technical performance.

✅ The selected configuration is recommended for detailed design and construction of hydraulic structures.
