✅ Conclusion

The Gumbasa Irrigation System rehabilitation and reconstruction project is economically and financially viable. The Economic Internal Rate of Return (14.2%) and Financial IRR (10.7%) both exceed the 9% opportunity cost of capital. The project generates significant incremental farm income, expands command area by 2,339 hectares, and improves cropping intensity. Sensitivity analysis shows the project remains feasible under most adverse scenarios except a 10% drop in crop prices. The positive ENPV, FNPV, and BCR ratios confirm the project delivers high value to the region and stakeholders.
